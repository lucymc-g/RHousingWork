About

Background

This site collects R-based analysis work in housing policy, fair housing, and community data. Projects draw on public datasets including ACS microdata via IPUMS, HMDA, and locally-collected survey data.

Methods include survey analysis, spatial mapping, and exploratory data work oriented toward low- and moderate-income populations and historically underserved communities.
